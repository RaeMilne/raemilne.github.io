A system for sharing process and project documentation

Moment’s extranet is used to share work with clients and collaborators. The page templates hadn’t been updated in several years, and previous attempts to modernize the system had been unsuccessful. Working in week-long agile sprints, my partner and I created user-friendly templates based on Markdown that are easy to use and reflect Moment’s recent brand refresh. We also wrote extensive help resources and crafted best practices for project documentation.

Contribution: user research, concept sketching, wireframing, visual design, usability testing, help documentation, best practices

An intimate social space for teens and young adults with cancer

Our team designed a private social network enabling teens and young adults with cancer to connect with each other, share their experiences, and get reliable answers to their questions from medical experts. The app was designed for for a major cancer treatment and research center to complement a physical lounge and event programming specifically geared towards a young audience.

Contribution: research synthesis, competitive audit, concept sketching, wireframing, usability testing, visual refinement and extension

A personalized platform for finding your dream home

Our client was an aspiring real estate startup whose agents were dedicated to representing homebuyers, never sellers. The responsive digital platform we designed aimed to provide more relevant, personalized property matches to homebuyers based on their profile and preferences.

Contribution: Workshop facilitation, product strategy, user research, service blueprinting, data-driven content strategy, concept sketching, wireframing, documentation

A content strategy for a dynamic homepage

We helped a major media company define a new direction for their homepage. Beginning with a participatory workshop, we outlined the goals of key stakeholders and led a series of ideation exercises that subsequently informed our design and content strategy recommendations.

Contribution: workshop facilitation, concept sketching, wireframing, content strategy

A tool for launching marketing campaigns

A major financial institution used internal technology to manage reward campaigns for their line of consumer credit cards. Their original software was broken up into multiple tools developed by engineers over two decades. We were tasked with consolidating and updating the software. Our process began with an extensive user-centered research phase where we audited the current toolset and the workflows of multiple users to identify pain points and opportunities.

Contribution: User research and synthesis, workshop facilitation, product strategy, concept sketching, wireframing, visual refinement and extension, documentation
